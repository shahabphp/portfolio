Startup founders

Investors

Product leaders

Technology companies

## First Impression

"This person builds serious products."

"This person leads teams."

"This person understands business."

"This person can execute."

Confident

Calm

Professional

Modern

Curious

Innovative

Technical

Strategic

## Core Message

Technology should solve business problems.

Leadership matters.

Execution matters.

Great products are built through thoughtful engineering and product thinking.

---

## Writing Style

Clear.

Confident.

Short sentences.

Avoid buzzwords.

Avoid exaggeration.

Prefer evidence over claims.

Use numbers whenever possible.

Explain impact rather than responsibilities.

---

## Storytelling

Every section should answer one question.

Who am I?

What problems do I solve?

How do I think?

What have I achieved?

Why should someone work with me?

What should the visitor do next?
