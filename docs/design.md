# Design Principles

## Overall Style

Minimal.

Premium.

Elegant.

Editorial.

Timeless.

Dark-first.

Professional.

---

## Inspiration

Use inspiration from:

Apple

Linear

Vercel

Stripe

Raycast

Figma

Awwwards

Never imitate.

Only learn from their principles.

---

## Typography

Typography is the primary visual element.

Use strong hierarchy.

Generous whitespace.

Large headings.

Readable paragraphs.

Avoid decorative fonts.

Use font weight intentionally.

---

## Layout

Content-first.

Asymmetrical when appropriate.

Strong spacing rhythm.

8px spacing system.

Wide layouts.

Consistent alignment.

Avoid cramped sections.

---

## Colors

Dark background.

One primary accent color.

High contrast.

Subtle gradients only.

Never rely on color alone.

---

## Components

Simple.

Reusable.

Consistent.

Accessible.

Purposeful.

Avoid visual noise.

---

## Images

Use authentic visuals.

Avoid stock photography.

Prefer screenshots, diagrams, product visuals, and custom illustrations.

---

## UX

Every section should have a clear purpose.

Reduce cognitive load.

Avoid unnecessary scrolling.

Every interaction should feel intentional.

---

## Success Criteria

The design should feel premium without trying to impress through complexity.

Quality should come from clarity, spacing, typography, and attention to detail.
