# Personal Portfolio

## Mission

Build a world-class personal portfolio website for Shahab Pakshekarestalkhi.

The goal is not simply to showcase projects, but to establish Shahab as a trusted Technical Executive, Product Leader, Entrepreneur, and Full-Stack Engineer.

Every design, interaction, animation, and engineering decision should reinforce professionalism, technical excellence, product thinking, and business impact.

# Workflow

Before implementing any major feature:

1. Explain the idea.
2. Explain why it improves the product.
3. Mention possible alternatives when appropriate.
4. Then implement.

Do not immediately generate code for large features.

---

# Design Review Process

When designing a new page or major section:

1. Present three distinct creative directions.
2. Explain the strengths and trade-offs of each.
3. Recommend one direction and justify the choice.
4. Wait for approval before implementing the final version.

Do not assume the first idea is the best idea. Explore alternatives before committing to a design.

---

# Design Goal

Aim for craftsmanship comparable to an Awwwards Honorable Mention.

Do not imitate award-winning websites.

Instead apply their underlying principles:

• Outstanding typography
• Strong storytelling
• Clear hierarchy
• Editorial layouts
• Smooth interactions
• Excellent performance
• Premium attention to detail

Originality is more important than visual complexity.

---

# Engineering Principles

Always prefer:

Maintainability

Accessibility

Performance

Readability

Scalability

Developer Experience

Avoid clever solutions when a simpler one is equally effective.

---

# General Rules

Never use JavaScript.

Always use TypeScript.

Prefer Server Components.

Prefer semantic HTML.

Prefer composition over inheritance.

Everything should be reusable.

Avoid unnecessary dependencies.

Accessibility is mandatory.

SEO is mandatory.

Responsive design is mandatory.

Performance is mandatory.

---

# Quality Checklist

Every page should:

Communicate a clear purpose.

Load quickly.

Work on every screen size.

Have meaningful animations.

Feel polished.

Avoid visual clutter.

Tell part of the overall story.

---

# Anti-Patterns

Avoid:

Generic portfolio templates

Skill bars

Fake loading screens

Decorative animations

Meaningless 3D objects

Overuse of glassmorphism

Overuse of gradients

Copying existing websites

Animations that slow navigation

Design decisions without purpose

---

Always think about the experience from the visitor's perspective.

Every decision should improve communication, usability, or delight.
